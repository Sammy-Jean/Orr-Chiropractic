What we do

We are dedicated and experienced professionals who create individualized treatment plans and foster an open and welcoming environment to ensure a positive experience towards an enhanced health wellness lifestyle.

Services Overview

Our staff believes chiropractic care, massage therapy, and dry needling are essential and highly effective care to address chronic pain or injuries. Each of our patients undergoes various assessments to identify underlying issues resulting in pain or discomfort and receives an individualized treatment plan for restoration.

Chiropractic Care Details

Treatments and adjustments include mobility techniques, like manual manipulation, mobilization, and spinal decompression to facilitate pain management. These tools and techniques are proven to improve:

  • body function and mobility
  • Immune system function
  • Back Pain
  • Neck Pain
  • Headaches
  • Joint Pain

Massage Therapy

Massage therapy activates and stimulates soft tissue through various techniques to promote relaxation, relieve pain, improve circulation, and enhance overall well-being. It is non-invasive, drug-free, and can be used with other forms of treatment. We offer various massages to help relax, heal, and restore the body. Each massage specialty provides relief from:

  • Muscle Tenstion
  • Stress & Anxiety
  • Swedish
  • Deep Tissue
  • Reflexology

Dry Needling

Dry needling is a therapeutic technique that uses thin needles to release trigger points in muscles, tendons, or ligaments, alleviating pain and improving mobility. It increases blood flow and is often used with other forms of treatment for holistic care.
