Who We Are

Established in 2015, Orr Chiropractic & Massage is a holistic, family-based chiropractic and massage clinic with a professional staff with over 15 years of experience. The practice is dedication to creating and facilitating an open and inclusive environment for healing and restoration.

Our Services

Chiropractic Care

Chiropractic care treats musculoskeletal disorders with hands-on techniques to restore proper alignment and function, alleviating pain and improving mobility. It's non-invasive, drug-free, and enhances the body's natural ability to heal itself.

Massage Therapy

Massage therapy manipulates soft tissues to promote relaxation, relieve pain, improve circulation, and enhance overall well-being. It is non-invasive, drug-free, and can be used with other forms of treatment.

Dry Needling

Dry needling is a therapeutic technique that uses thin needles to release trigger points in muscles, tendons, or ligaments, alleviating pain and improving mobility. It increases blood flow and is often used with other forms of treatment for holistic care.
